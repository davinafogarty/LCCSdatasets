Famous Jungles Around the World

Explore the most iconic jungles on our planet!

Amazon Rainforest

The Amazon Rainforest is the largest tropical rainforest in the world, spanning multiple countries in South America. It is home to diverse wildlife and plant species.

Congo Rainforest

Located in Central Africa, the Congo Rainforest is the second-largest rainforest on Earth and a crucial carbon sink for the planet.

Daintree Rainforest

Found in Queensland, Australia, the Daintree Rainforest is one of the oldest tropical rainforests, estimated to be over 135 million years old.
